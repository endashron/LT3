My Top 10 Favorite Movies

Top 1

Shameless (2011–2021) is a comedy-drama about the chaotic Gallagher family in Chicago, led by their alcoholic father, Frank. His kids struggle to survive, facing poverty, relationships, and personal challenges with dark humor and resilience.

Top 2

Aladdin is a fantasy tale about a poor but kind-hearted boy who finds a magic lamp with a powerful genie. With its help, he seeks love, adventure, and a better life while facing an evil sorcerer.

Top 3

Shadow and Bone is a fantasy series about Alina Starkov, a young soldier who discovers she has rare magical powers. As she navigates a world of dark forces and political intrigue, she becomes key to a battle between light and shadow.

Top 4

Batman is a superhero story about Bruce Wayne, a billionaire who fights crime in Gotham City using his intellect, combat skills, and gadgets. Driven by the loss of his parents, he becomes the Dark Knight to bring justice.

Top 5

Alien: Romulus (2024) is a sci-fi horror film about young space colonists facing deadly creatures on a derelict station.

Top 6

Black Panther is a superhero story about T’Challa, king of Wakanda, who fights to protect his nation using advanced technology and warrior skills.

Top 7

The Silence of the Lambs* is a thriller about an FBI trainee, Clarice Starling, who seeks help from the brilliant but deadly Dr. Hannibal Lecter to catch a serial killer.

Top 8

Smile (2022) is a psychological horror film about a therapist who experiences terrifying visions after witnessing a patient's bizarre suicide, leading her to uncover a sinister curse.

Top 9

Guardians of the Galaxy is a sci-fi adventure about a misfit team of space criminals who unite to protect the galaxy from powerful threats.

Top 10

The Notebook is a romantic drama about a young couple, Noah and Allie, whose love is tested by class differences and fate but endures over the years.
